Yakshagana: A Decade of Devotion

Mastering the Legacy : My Ever-Growing Journey in Yakshagana

For the past 11 years, I have been passionately immersed in mastering the Badagu Thittu style of Yakshagana—beginning my training under the renowned Guru Krishnamurthy Thunga and continuing to refine my skills under the expert guidance of Guru Srinidhi Holla. My journey as a devoted student of this mesmerizing art form is ever-evolving, fueling my dedication to lifelong learning and artistic growth.

Mridanga: The Heartbeat of Indian Classical Music

Classical Discipline

Parallel to his theatrical pursuits, Purujith has dedicated 11 years to mastering the classical Mridanga, the quintessential percussion instrument of Carnatic music. My voyage in the world of Mridanga began under the guidance of Guru Jaychandra Rao, followed by enriching mentorship with Guru Shashi Kumar. I am currently deepening my mastery and artistic expression under the illustrious Ganakala Shri Guru Anoor Anantha Krishna Sharma, allowing my rhythmic journey to flourish with each beat.
